About Us

A few words about us

Precision Agriculture: EzeAgro leverages an amalgamation of cutting-edge technologies, including IoT, AI, ML, Edge and Cloud Computing, to revolutionize precision agriculture. This integrated approach allows farmers to achieve unprecedented levels of precision and efficiency in their farming practices.
Real-time Precision Farming: Leveraging IoT, ML, and telemetry, EzeAgro's sensor network empowers farmers with rapid, data-driven decisions on irrigation and fertilization, enhancing agricultural efficiency.
Comprehensive Agricultural Management: Through IoT, ML, AI, and cloud-edge computing, EzeAgro offers holistic farm management. It includes crop health monitoring, livestock tracking, and real-time data capture, optimizing production.
NPK Detection via IoT and Image Processing: Employing IoT sensors and image analysis enhanced by ML and AI, we aim to develop a sustainable solution for real-time NPK detection in fields, optimizing traditional agriculture with computer vision and deep learning.
Data-Driven Decisions: Leveraging the prowess of Power BI and advanced technologies like ML, AI, and Deep Learning, we aim to analyze and derive actionable insights from the copious data generated by farm field.
AgriTech Monitoring Platform: This user-friendly web and mobile app, powered by HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, React, SQL, and Node.js, offers real-time field data visualization. Integrated with IoT sensors via RESTful APIs, it ensures accessibility. ML analytics optimize farming practices, and it supports 32 languages for user convenience.

Why we are different

EzeAgro pioneers precision agriculture with IoT, ML, and real-time data insights. Our sensor network empowers farmers for efficient irrigation and fertilization. Comprehensive farm management optimizes crop health and livestock tracking. We're innovators, holding patents, winning three hackathons, and leading the field with our user-friendly AgriTech platform.
